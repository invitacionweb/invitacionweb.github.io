Travel Tips!!

• Getting to Guadalajara:
Fly to Guadalajara International Airport

• Getting to your hotel:
Taxi: Prepaid stands inside the airport
Ride sharing services like Uber are not allowed at the airport.

• Suggested hotels:
- Hyatt Regency Andares
- Hard Rock Hotel
- Hotel Riu Plaza Guadalajara
- One Guadalajara Periférico Norte (Closest to the venue)

• Getting to the wedding:
We kindly suggest allowing extra travel time to reach the ceremony, as Saturday traffic may be heavier than usual. For your convenience, you may consider using private ride sharing services.

• Weather (March):
Daytime highs usually reach 29 ºC (84 ºF), while nights can drop to about 9–10 ºC (48–50 ºF).

• Money and Payments:
Most places accept credit cards. But carry some cash (Mexican pesos) for street food, markets, and small shops.

• Must-see spots:
Tour Historic downtown Guadalajara with the “Tapatío Tour” (www.tapatiotour.com.mx):
- Cathedral
- Teatro Degollado
- Hospicio Cabañas
- Plaza de los Mariachis

• Other must-see areas:
- Tequila – Tour: “Tequila Express” (www.tequilaexpress.mx)
- Tlaquepaque – Tour: “Tapatío Tour” (www.tapatiotour.com.mx)
- Chapala – Tour: “Tapatío Tour” (www.tapatiotour.com.mx)

• Trendy areas:
- Colonia Americana
- Chapultepec Avenue
